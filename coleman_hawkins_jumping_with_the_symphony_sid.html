Jumping with the Symphony Sid

Brief

Song written about longtime jazz disc jockey Sid Torin (originally Sidney Tarnopol), who many have credited with bring bebop to a mass audience. There are conflicting stories about how Torin came by the nickname "Symphony Sid".
