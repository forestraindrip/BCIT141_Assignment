Welcome to Jazz Live!

What is Jazz Live

Jazz Live is a place to share the beauty of jazz music to everyone who is interested in jazz music. No matter you are an jazz collector or a jazz beginner, this is the place for you.

The website provides information of talented jazz players around the world and their discography with featured recordings. In addition, We also have free newsletter published fornightly. Don't miss the opportunity to follow the latest news of jazz music!
